DIGITAL MANUSCRIPTS

PMI has partnered with the Hill Museum and Manuscript Library (HMML) to display and annotate the digital manuscripts produced in our projects through their new vHMML Reading Room. No manuscripts are available yet for public viewing. We do expect, however, to release the first twenty Library of Congress Persian manuscripts through vHMML by the spring of 2017 (check out the sneak peak at one of these new digital Persian manuscripts from Library of Congress in vHMML on the left!).
